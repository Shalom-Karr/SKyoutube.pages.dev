Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [October 22, 2025]

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Shalom Karr and YH Studios ("we," "us," or "our") collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the SK Video Dashboard (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner.

2. Information We Collect

To provide and improve the Service, we may collect the following types of information:

2.1 Data Provided During Use

  • Video Details: When you save a video, we store its title and URL.
  • User Activity: We log timestamps of your interactions with the Service for administrative and security purposes.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP Address: We collect your IP address to help diagnose problems, administer our Service, and for security measures such as preventing malicious activity and enforcing our terms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • To monitor the usage of the Service and detect technical issues.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraudulent or unauthorized activity.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

To provide the Service, we store data in two distinct locations: in your browser and on our secure servers. It is important to understand the difference.

4.1 In Your Browser (Local Storage)

Your primary list of "Saved Videos" and "Followed Channels" is stored exclusively in your browser's Local Storage. This data remains on your device, is controlled by you, and is not stored or synchronized on our servers. Clearing your browser's data will permanently remove these lists.

Additionally, to enhance performance, data retrieved from the YouTube API (such as recent videos from channels you follow) is temporarily cached here. In compliance with the YouTube API Services Developer Policies, this cached data is automatically invalidated if it is older than 30 days.

4.2 On Our Servers (Supabase Logs)

For security and administrative reasons, we maintain a log of specific activities on the Service. When you save a video or follow a channel, an entry is created in our secure cloud database (Supabase). This log entry contains:

  • Your IP address
  • The URL and title of the video or channel you added
  • The timestamp of the action

This event log is separate from your personal saved lists. We use this data strictly to monitor for service abuse (such as automated spamming), diagnose technical problems, and ensure our application is operating correctly. We do not use this log to track your personal viewing habits or build a profile of you, and we do not sell or share this data with third parties.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation.
  • Protect and defend our rights or property.
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Service.
  • Protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public.

6. Third-Party Services (YouTube/Google)

Our Service utilizes the YouTube API Services to search for and display video content. By using features that access YouTube content, you are agreeing to be bound by the Google Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review it to understand how Google collects and processes your data. While we do not share your personally identifiable information (such as your IP address or saved video lists) directly with YouTube or Google through our use of their API, your interaction with embedded YouTube players and content is governed by their respective policies.

7. Your Choices and Rights

You can manage some of your data by clearing your browser's local storage. If you are a logged-in administrator, you have access to view and delete log entries. For any requests regarding your personal data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at shalomkarrsphone@gmail.com.
